AVIS Annual Dinner

Andover Town House

April 9, 2024

6 PM

Annual Dinner - Wardens and Volunteers Only Pricing


The Andover Village Improvement Society will hold its Annual Meeting at the Andover Town House on Tuesday, April 9, 2024 beginning at 6 PM. Cocktails and self-serve appetizers start at 6 PM with a cash bar followed by a buffet style dinner at 6:30. The Annual Meeting Agenda will begin at approximately 7 PM. Following the annual meeting Agenda will be speaker Meg Holmes. Meg is an Andover resident and avid hiker who will discuss traversing the Bay Circuit Trail and how it compares to hiking in other countries.


The cost to participate in the dinner portion of the event is $25 per person as a member/volunteer for AVIS, and payment is required to reserve your seat. Requests to refund or transfer tickets must be made prior to March 22, 2024. After this date, all tickets are non-transferable and non-refundable.
